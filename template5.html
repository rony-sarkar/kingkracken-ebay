Product Description

Defiant Key Features

High end materials and attention to detail makes the Defiant a step above the rest for appearance, reliability, and comfort. On the trail or on the road, the Defiant handles it all beautifully.



All-Terrain Fat Tire

The Defiant's 4" x 20" all-terrain fat tire powers through snow, sand, mud, or whatever your trail throws at you. Sturdy knobs on wheels are perfect for both on and off-road riding.



Concealed Removable Battery

Charge things up when you're on the go. The Defiant features a removable battery hidden in the center frame that locks with a key. Keep it safely stored, or take it out and recharge, worry-free.



Twist Throttle Grip

Get power on-demand with the variable speed twist throttle grip. High torque power is available on demand! Press the button under the left grip for loud electric horn.



Bright 5 Speed Display

Easy to read even in full daylight, the Defiant's LCD display shows you your speed, distance traveled, pedal assist level, and battery life all in a clean design. Choose between 5 levels of Pedal Assist.



4 Bulb LED Headlight

Bright adjustable front headlight for low light conditions. Powered from the Defiants's main Lithium Ion battery, improves visibility & safety.



7 Speed Shimano Derailleur

The Defiant features the 7 Speed Shimano Tourney Derailleur for manually pedaling your bike. Use the 7 Speed Thumb Shift Lever to rapidly shift between the gears on the rear cassette.



Quick Release Front Wheel

Quickly detach the front wheel for quick storage and transportation options.



Front and Rear Disc Brakes

Superior performance and smooth stopping power. The Defiant's dual 160mm stainless steel disc brakes provide even, reliable braking.



Dual Spring Saddle with Light

Dual spring suspension, mesh top, middle opening for breathability, and a bright red LED taillight with 3 modes. The Defiant comes stock with the amazing JupiterBike Upgrade Seat.



750 Watt High Torque Motor

Dominate any terrain and climb hills effortlessly with this ultra powerful 750w geared hub motor. A major boost to the enjoyment of your ride.



Front & Rear Fender Mudguards

These fenders make a tremendous difference when you are riding on roads that are wet from drizzle, recent rain, or snowmelt.

*Fenders not included on refurbished Defiant bikes.



Rear Cargo Rack*

Take your cargo with you with the Defiant Rear Cargo Rack. Built tough from aluminum alloy and mounted directly to the Defiant frame. A must have for any commuter. *Optional accessory
